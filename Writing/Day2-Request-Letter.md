# Day 2 · 请求信 (Request Letter)

请求信和投诉信很像：都是「说明情况 + 你希望对方做什么」。区别是：投诉是「出了问题要解决」，请求是「有事想请对方帮忙/提供信息/允许」。

---

## 结构 (Structure)

| 段落 | 内容 | 大约字数 |
|------|------|----------|
| **开头** | 你是谁 + 为什么写信（I am writing to ask about / to request...） | 2–3 句 |
| **第 2 段** | 背景/情况（为什么需要这次请求） | 2–3 句 |
| **第 3 段** | 具体请求（你希望对方做什么、提供什么） | 2–3 句 |
| **第 4 段**（可选）| 为什么重要 / 什么时候需要 | 1–2 句 |
| **结尾** | 感谢 + 期待回复（I look forward to your reply.） | 1–2 句 |

总目标：**150 字左右**。

---

## 称呼与结尾

- 不知道对方姓名：**Dear Sir or Madam,** / **Dear Customer Service,** → 结尾 **Yours faithfully,**
- 知道对方姓名：**Dear Mr Smith,** → 结尾 **Yours sincerely,**

---

## 模板句 (Useful phrases)

- 开头：I am writing to **ask for** / **request** / **inquire about** ...
- 说明原因：I would like to ... because ... / The reason I am writing is that ...
- 具体请求：I would be grateful if you could ... / Could you please ... / I would like you to ...
- 结尾：I look forward to your reply. / Thank you for your help.

## 你的另一稿批改（投诉+请求：经理冤枉未付款、要求删视频并道歉）

**原文问题汇总**：

| 原文 | 问题 | 改正 |
|------|------|------|
| beacuse | 拼写 | because |
| wrting | 拼写 | writing |
| insisted **in** I didn't pay | 搭配错误 | insisted **that** I hadn't paid / insisted I didn't pay |
| **Even** I ask him | 缺 when + 时态 | **Even when** I **asked** him |
| proved I **don't** steal | 时态一致（过去） | proved (that) I **didn't** steal |
| **lose my temperature** | 用词错误 | **lose my temper**（发脾气） |
| I am looking forward **your** reply | 缺介词 | I am looking forward **to** your reply |
| **Sincerely customer** | 格式 | **Sincerely,** 换行再写 Nu |

**小提示**：请求别人做某事常用 **ask sb to do** / **have sb do**；"let the manager delete" 可改为 **ask the manager to delete** 或 **have the manager delete**，更正式。

**改后完整版**：

```
Dear Customer Service,

I am writing to request an apology and the removal of a video posted by your manager, following a misunderstanding.

I had already paid for my goods, but the manager insisted that I had not paid. Even when I asked him to check the monitor history, and proved that I had not stolen the goods, he still refused to apologise to me. This made me lose my temper, and I eventually had to call the police for help. He also posted a video on TikTok, which has disturbed my daily life.

I would be grateful if you could ask the manager to delete the video and to make a formal apology to me.

I look forward to your reply. Please let me know if you need any further information.

Yours faithfully,
Nu
```

（英式拼写：apologise；美式：apologize。全文统一即可。）

## 改后版本大概能拿几分？（投诉+请求这封）

**说明**：只有考官打的才是正式分数；这里是自评参考。

| 维度 | 改后版大致表现 | 自估区间 |
|------|----------------|----------|
| Task Achievement | 情况说明清楚，两个请求（删视频、道歉）都写了 | 7–8 |
| Coherence & Cohesion | 分段清楚，有 Even when / I would be grateful 等 | 6.5–7 |
| Lexical Resource | 用词正确（temper, insist that, look forward to）；无难词但够用 | 6.5–7 |
| Grammatical Range & Accuracy | 改后无语法错误，时态一致（had paid, asked, had not stolen） | 6.5–7 |

**综合自估**：改后这封在 **6.5–7** 之间合理；考场少出错的话 **冲 7 有机会**。

**关于「误触」**：beacuse、wrting 这类属于拼写/打字错误，考试时留 2–3 分钟检查能改掉不少。**lose my temperature** 是词汇选择问题（应为 lose my temper），不是误触，这个搭配可以记一下。
