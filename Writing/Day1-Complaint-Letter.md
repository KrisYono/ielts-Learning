# Day 1 · 投诉信 (Complaint Letter)

## 题目

You recently bought an item from an online store. When it arrived, it was damaged.  
Write a letter to the company. In your letter:  
- describe what you bought and the damage  
- say what you want them to do  
- explain why it is important to you  

---

## 结构 (Structure)

| 段落 | 内容 | 大约字数 |
|------|------|----------|
| **开头** | 你是谁 + 为什么写信（I am writing to complain about...） | 2–3 句 |
| **第 2 段** | 回应 bullet 1：买了什么、损坏情况 | 3–4 句 |
| **第 3 段** | 回应 bullet 2：你希望他们做什么（换货/退款/道歉） | 2–3 句 |
| **第 4 段** | 回应 bullet 3：为什么对你重要（急用/送礼/唯一设备） | 2 句 |
| **结尾** | 期待回复、留联系方式（I look forward to your reply.） | 1–2 句 |

总目标：**150 字左右**（略超可以，不要写太少）。

---

## 称呼 (Salutation) — 开头怎么写

❌ **不要写**：Dear online store company / Dear company  
- 太生硬，也不符合英文信件习惯。

✅ **应该这样**（不知道对方姓名时）：
- **Dear Sir or Madam,**（正式）
- **Dear Customer Service,**（常用，推荐）

知道对方名字时可以用 **Dear Mr Smith,** 等。投诉信通常不知道具体负责人，用上面两个之一即可。  
后面要加**逗号**，然后换行写正文。

---

## 模板 (Template) — 照着套

```
Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to complain about [一件什么事]. I [什么时候买的/收到的].

[第 2 段：买了什么 + 损坏]
I ordered [具体物品] from your website on [日期]. When it arrived, I found that [损坏 1]. In addition, [损坏 2]. [如有第三点：Furthermore, ...].

[第 3 段：你希望他们做什么]
I would like you to [refund / replace / send a new one]. I believe this is reasonable given the condition of the product.

[第 4 段：为什么对你重要]
This is particularly important to me because [原因：e.g. I bought it for a special occasion / I need it for work]. [再一句补充].

I look forward to your reply. Please contact me at [邮箱或电话].

Yours faithfully,
[你的名字]
```

**结尾**：不知道对方姓名时用 **Yours faithfully,**（英式）；美式可用 Sincerely,。  
知道对方姓名时用 **Yours sincerely,**。

## 你的例子改一版 + 语法提示

你写的要点（台球 break cue、Predator 品牌、杆身断、后端刮痕、保护套丢失、要退款、女友生日礼物）都很好，下面按模板整理成一版，并标出容易错的地方：

**称呼**：Dear Sir or Madam,

**第 1 段**：  
I am writing to complain about an order I received from your store. The item arrived damaged and incomplete.

**第 2 段**（买了什么 + 损坏）：  
I bought a Predator billiards break cue from your website. When I got the delivery, I found that the shaft was broken and the back end had some obvious scratches. The protector had also been lost (or: was missing) when the package arrived.

**语法提示**：  
- “the back end **have**” → **had**（单数主语用 has/had）。  
- “when I **get** the delivery” → “when I **got**”（过去时一致：收到是过去的事）。  
- “girlfrend” → **girlfriend**（拼写）。

**第 3 段**（你希望他们做什么）：  
I would like you to refund my payment. I believe this is reasonable given the condition of the product.

**第 4 段**（为什么对你重要）：  
This is important to me because I bought it for my girlfriend’s birthday. In its current state, it cannot be used as a present.

**结尾**：  
I look forward to your reply. Please contact me at [your email or phone].

Yours faithfully,  
[Your name]

## 自检清单 (Checklist)

- [ ] 单复数一致（the item was / the items were）
- [ ] 第三人称 -s（the package arrives → it arrives）
- [ ] 时态正确（买了用过去时，现在状态用现在时）
- [ ] 没有残句（每句都有主谓）
- [ ] 三个 bullet 都写到了

## 你的一稿批改 (Your draft — corrected)

**原文问题汇总**（考官会扣分的小错）：

| 原文 | 问题 | 改正 |
|------|------|------|
| Dear customer service | 称呼首字母大写 | Dear Customer Service, |
| delivery broken issue | 表达略怪 | the damaged item I received / a delivery problem |
| In addtion | 拼写 | In addition, |
| the back end of cue | 缺冠词 | the back end of **the** cue |
| the back end ... **have** | 主谓一致 + 时态 | the back end ... **had** |
| reseasonable | 拼写 | reasonable |
| imporntant | 拼写 | important |
| planed | 拼写（plan 双写 n） | planned |
| And this color... | 正式信少用句首 And | 改为另起一句不加 And，或合并到上句 |
| Sincerely,Nu | 格式 | Sincerely, 换行再写 Nu |

**改后完整版**（可直接当范文对照）：

```
Dear Customer Service,

I am writing to complain about the damaged item I received. I ordered a Predator break cue on your website on March 5th. When it arrived, I found that the shaft of the cue had been broken. In addition, the back end of the cue had some scratches.

I would like you to send a new one to me. I believe this is reasonable given the condition of the product.

This is particularly important to me because I planned to use this cue as my girlfriend's present, and this colour is her favourite.

I look forward to your reply. My contact number is +1 778 320 1022. Please let me know if you need any further information.

Yours sincerely,
Nu
```

**小提示**：英式拼写 colour / favourite 在 IELTS 可接受；美式用 color / favorite。全文统一一种即可。  
**电话格式**：+1 778 320 1022 中间加空格更清晰（非必须）。
