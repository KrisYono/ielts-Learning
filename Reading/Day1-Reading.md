## 短文 (Passage) — 自编练习，非真题

**The Riverside Museum**

The Riverside Museum in Glasgow opened in 2011. It is one of the most visited museums in Scotland. The building was designed by Zaha Hadid and sits next to the River Clyde. Inside, the museum has a large collection of objects related to transport and travel: cars, buses, trains, and ships. Many of these were made or used in Scotland.

The museum is free to enter. It is open every day except 25 December and 1 January. Visitors can also use the café and the shop. Schools and groups are asked to book in advance. The museum has won several awards for its design and for the way it shows the history of transport.

---

## 题目：True / False / Not Given

根据上文判断下列句子。选 **TRUE**（与文章一致）、**FALSE**（与文章矛盾）或 **NOT GIVEN**（文章未提及）。

1. The Riverside Museum is in Scotland.  
2. The museum was designed by a British architect.  
3. Visitors must pay to enter the museum.  
4. The museum is closed on 1 January.

---

## 答案与简要说明 (Answers)

| 题号 | 答案 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 1 | **TRUE** | 第一段：Glasgow 在苏格兰，且说 "one of the most visited museums in Scotland"。 |
| 2 | **NOT GIVEN** | 文章只说了 "designed by Zaha Hadid"，没有说她是 British 还是别的国籍。 |
| 3 | **FALSE** | 第二段明确说 "The museum is free to enter."，和 "must pay" 矛盾。 |
| 4 | **TRUE** | 第二段："closed ... on ... 1 January"。 |

---

## T/F/NG 小技巧（今天先记住两条）

- **FALSE**：文章里有信息，且和题目**相反**（如文章说 free，题目说 must pay）。
- **NOT GIVEN**：文章**没提**题目问的那件事（如没提国籍，就不能选 T 或 F，只能 NG）。
