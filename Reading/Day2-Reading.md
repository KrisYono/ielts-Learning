## 短文 (Passage)

**Working from Home**

Many companies now allow their staff to work from home at least one or two days per week. This practice became more common after 2020. Employees often say they save time and money by not travelling to the office. They also enjoy having more flexibility to look after family or hobbies.

However, some people find it harder to focus at home. There can be more distractions, and it is not always easy to separate work from personal life. Employers have different views: some believe that productivity stays the same or even improves when people work remotely, while others prefer to have everyone in the office.

---

## 题目：True / False / Not Given

1. Most companies allowed staff to work from home before 2020.  
2. Some employees think working from home saves them time.  
3. All employers believe that productivity is better when staff work from home.  
4. Separating work and personal life is sometimes difficult when working from home.

---

## 答案与说明 (Answers)

| 题号 | 答案 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 1 | **FALSE** | 第一段说 "became more common **after** 2020"，即 2020 之后才更普遍，和 "before 2020" 矛盾。 |
| 2 | **TRUE** | 第一段："Employees often say they **save time** and money by not travelling." |
| 3 | **FALSE** | 第二段："Employers have **different** views"；有的认为 productivity 一样或更好，有的 "prefer to have everyone in the office"，不是 "all" 都认为更好。 |
| 4 | **TRUE** | 第二段："it is not always **easy to separate work from personal life**." |

做完可对照上表，错题在 Progress 里写一句原因。
