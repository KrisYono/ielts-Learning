# Day 2 · Speaking — Part 2 练习

## 今天做什么（约 15 分钟）

1. **准备 1 分钟**：看话题卡，在纸上写 2–4 个关键词。
2. **说 1 分 30 秒～2 分钟**，录音后自听。
3. **目标**：说满 1:30，四个要点都提到。

---

## Part 2 话题卡 (Cue card)

**Describe a person who has helped you (at work, at school or in daily life).**

You should say:  
- **who** the person is  
- **how** you know them  
- **what** kind of help they gave you  
- and **why** their help was important to you  

---

## 结构提示（不必背）

| 部分 | 可说的内容 | 大约时间 |
|------|------------|----------|
| **开头** | The person I’d like to talk about is ... / I’m going to describe someone who helped me. | 10 秒 |
| **who** | 家人/同事/老师/朋友，和你的关系 | 15 秒 |
| **how you know them** | 怎么认识的（同事、同学、邻居…） | 15 秒 |
| **what help** | 具体帮了什么（找工作、复习、搬家、建议…） | 30–40 秒 |
| **why important** | 对你当时/后来的影响 | 20 秒 |
| **结尾** | So I’m really grateful to them. / I still remember that. | 5 秒 |
